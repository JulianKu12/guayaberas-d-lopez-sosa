# Product Definition: Premium Real Estate

## Initial Concept
A premium real estate discovery and management platform focused on high-end properties.

## Vision
To provide a visually stunning and intuitive platform where home seekers can discover, search, and manage their favorite premium properties. The platform aims to match the elegance of high-end real estate with a seamless user experience.

## Target Users
- **Home Seekers/Buyers:** Individuals looking for premium, high-end properties who value visual storytelling and a smooth discovery process.

## Core Goals
- **Visual Storytelling:** Prioritize high-quality imagery and elegant UI design to create an immersive experience for luxury listings.
- **Search & Filters:** Empower users with powerful search and filtering tools to narrow down their ideal premium home.

## Key Features
- **Search & Filters:** A flexible search system with multiple filters for location, price, and property type.
- **Discovery Screen:** A curated home screen designed for discovering featured and new premium properties.
