# Product Guidelines: Premium Real Estate

## Prose Style: Elegant & Sophisticated
- **Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, and modern.
- **Voice:** Direct but approachable, using clear language that reflects the premium nature of the listings.
- **Copy Goals:** Inspire confidence and help users navigate high-end properties with ease.

## UX Principles: Focus on Simplicity
- **Simplicity First:** Prioritize a clutter-free interface. Focus on one major action at a time to ensure the user isn't overwhelmed by options.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Use typography and spacing to create a clear visual hierarchy, guiding the user's eye to the most important property details.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Ensure all search and filter actions are easy to find and use, providing instant feedback.

## Branding & Visual Identity: Earthy & Premium Greens
- **Primary Colors:** Use the established palette including "Nordic" (#19322F) and "Mosque" (#006655).
- **Secondary Colors:** Support the primary greens with "Hint Green" (#D9ECC8) and clean backgrounds like "Clear Day" (#EEF6F6).
- **Typography:** A combination of "Newsreader" (serif) for a classic, sophisticated look and "SF Pro" (sans-serif) for clean, readable interface elements.
- **Imagery:** Use high-quality, professional photography for all listings, ensuring they are the focal point of the discovery screens.
